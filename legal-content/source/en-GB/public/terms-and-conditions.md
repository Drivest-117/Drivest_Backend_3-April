9. Instructor Marketplace, Payments and Payouts
Where instructor marketplace features are available, Drivest acts as a platform and may act as a collection agent on behalf of instructors for the purpose of facilitating lesson payments.
Drivest retains an eight percent platform fee on successfully completed lessons. Drivest does not retain commission on cancelled lessons.
The finalised lesson cancellation model is as follows. If a learner cancels forty-eight hours or more before the scheduled lesson, the learner receives a full refund. If cancellation takes place between twenty-four and forty-eight hours before the lesson, up to twenty-five percent may be retained by the instructor as a cancellation fee. If cancellation takes place between twelve and twenty-four hours before the lesson, up to fifty percent may be retained by the instructor. If cancellation takes place less than twelve hours before the lesson, no refund is due. If the instructor cancels, the learner receives a full refund. If the learner fails to attend, the booking may be treated as a late cancellation or no-show and no refund may be issued.
