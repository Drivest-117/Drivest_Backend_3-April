Frequently Asked Questions
What does Drivest provide?
Drivest provides learning tools, reconstructed practice routes, advisory navigation support, hazard prompts, parking guidance, progress tracking, subscriptions, and, where enabled, instructor marketplace and booking features.
Is Drivest affiliated with DVSA?
No. Drivest is an independent product and has no affiliation with the Driver and Vehicle Standards Agency or any government authority.
Are Drivest routes official driving test routes?
No. Routes are reconstructed or generated for learning and practice only and must never be described as official test routes.
Does Drivest guarantee I will pass my driving test?
No. Drivest supports preparation and structured practice only. It does not guarantee any driving test outcome.
Can I rely on Drivest as the final authority while driving?
No. Users must always follow live road signs, real-world conditions, traffic laws, and any instruction from an instructor, examiner, police officer, or other authority.
Does Drivest guarantee parking legality or availability?
No. Parking outputs are advisory only. Users must check local signage and local restrictions before leaving a vehicle.
What subscription plans does Drivest offer?
Drivest offers an annual navigation plan, an annual navigation plan bundled with one month of practice route access for one selected test centre, and a monthly practice routes plan. The exact features and duration are shown at the point of purchase.
Are subscriptions automatically renewed?
Yes. Subscriptions renew automatically unless cancelled through the relevant app store or payment channel.
Do practice routes last for the full bundled annual subscription period?
Not always. Under the bundled annual navigation and limited practice route plan, navigation continues for the annual period but route access for the chosen centre expires after one month unless extended through a separate purchase.
How do subscription refunds work?
Subscription refunds are handled in accordance with the rules of the relevant app store or payment provider. Drivest does not guarantee subscription refunds.
How do lesson bookings work?
Where marketplace features are live, Drivest allows learners to discover instructors, select availability, book a lesson, and complete payment through the platform. The lesson itself remains a contract between the learner and the instructor.
Does Drivest take commission on cancelled lessons?
No. Drivest retains no commission on cancelled lessons.
How much commission does Drivest take on completed lessons?
Drivest retains an eight percent platform fee on completed lessons.
What is the lesson cancellation policy?
If a learner cancels forty-eight hours or more before the lesson, a full refund applies. Between twenty-four and forty-eight hours, up to twenty-five percent may be retained by the instructor. Between twelve and twenty-four hours, up to fifty percent may be retained. Less than twelve hours before the lesson, no refund applies. If the instructor cancels, the learner receives a full refund.
Does Drivest verify instructors?
Drivest may carry out limited checks for platform integrity, but it does not certify or guarantee instructor qualifications or status unless this is expressly stated.
Are instructors employed by Drivest?
No. Instructors are independent service providers and are responsible for their own lessons, licences, insurance, vehicle legality, conduct, and tax obligations.
What data does Drivest collect?
Depending on the features used, Drivest may process account details, device and technical data, learning progress, route activity, booking records, payment metadata, consent records, review content, dispute evidence, and audit logs.
Does Drivest track my location all the time?
No. Location is used where route, hazard, or parking features require it and where device permission is granted. Drivest does not store continuous background location history on its servers unless that is clearly disclosed in a future version.
Does Drivest use analytics?
Drivest may use analytics and diagnostic tools to improve performance and reliability. Where analytics relies on consent, it is enabled only after the user agrees.
Does Drivest sell my data?
No. Drivest does not sell personal data for advertising or marketing purposes.
How do disputes work?
A learner or instructor may submit a dispute through the platform where available. Drivest may review the available record, including logs, timestamps, payment data, and evidence, and may make a platform decision such as no action, refund, restriction, or suspension.
Is Drivest a mediator or legal authority?
No. Drivest provides a platform review process only and does not act as a court, tribunal, or legal arbitrator.
Can accounts be suspended?
Yes. Accounts may be restricted, suspended, or terminated where reasonably necessary for safety, fraud prevention, abuse control, legal risk, payment issues, or other serious platform concerns.
What happens if a third-party service fails?
Some features depend on third-party providers such as mapping services, payment systems, analytics tools, hosting services, or app stores. If one of those services fails, the related Drivest feature may be delayed, restricted, or unavailable.
How do I request access to or deletion of my data?
Users should email admin@drivest.uk with the request details. Identity verification may be required before action is taken.
