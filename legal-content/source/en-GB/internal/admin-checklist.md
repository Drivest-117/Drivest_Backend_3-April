ADMIN APPROVAL CHECKLIST

Use this checklist when reviewing instructor onboarding and marketplace visibility.

1. Verify identity and profile details
- Verify name and contact details are present and internally consistent.
- Check the profile is complete enough for review.
- Check area coverage, lesson format, pricing, and listed details for obvious inconsistencies or risk indicators.

2. Review instructor compliance position
- Check licence or ADI status if provided.
- Review insurance information if provided.
- Check vehicle legality and roadworthiness declarations if provided.
- Confirm the instructor has accepted the current instructor declaration and agreement.

3. Review legal and marketplace risk
- Check for misleading claims, unsupported guarantees, or suspicious profile content.
- Check whether any fraud, safety, payment, chargeback, or dispute flags are already attached to the account.
- Check whether a prior approval, restriction, or suspension record exists.

4. Set review outcome
- Approve
- Reject
- Request additional information
- Suspend or restrict where a live safety, fraud, or compliance concern exists

5. Record the decision
- Record reviewer identity.
- Record decision timestamp.
- Record reason code or review notes.
- Record the status applied to the instructor profile.
- Record whether the profile is approved for marketplace visibility.

6. Operational rule
- Do not approve marketplace visibility where the review is incomplete or materially inconsistent.
- If documents or declarations are missing, keep the profile pending or request more information rather than approving by assumption.
