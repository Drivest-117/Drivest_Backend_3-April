Hagidda Drivest waa mid talo bixin oo kaliya ah, waxaana laga yaabaa inay qabyo tahay ama ay dib u dhacdo.
Ha u aaminin app-kan sidii dammaanad ahaan.
Ha isticmaalin app-ka intaad baabuurka waddo.
Si buuxda u xakamee baabuurka, mar walbana raac calaamadaha waddada ee tooska ah, calaamadaha dhulka, iyo sharciyada taraafikada.
